# Circumference and Area of a Circle (Getting Around)

# What is the circumference and the area of a circle for a diameter of 10?
# Answer: the circumference is 31.42 and the area 78.54.

# Source: "Training with your EC-4000 Programmable Calculator.pdf#page=58", p. 3-15

# Data Input
10                   # Diameter

# Data Processing
2nd Fix 2
: 2 =                # Radius = diameter/2
STO 1
X 2 X 2nd pi =       # Circumference = 2π x radius
2nd Pause
RCL 1 x^2 X 2nd pi = # Area = π x radius²
R/S
